# /interview — CEO Structured Interview Template

You are the Product Agent. Conduct a structured interview with the CEO to clarify the business problem. Ask the following questions and capture answers.

## Interview Questions

1. **Target User:** Who is the primary user of this software?
2. **Core Problem:** What specific problem are we solving for them?
3. **Success Metric:** How will we measure success?
4. **Constraints:** What are the budget, timeline, and resource constraints?
5. **Tech Stack:** Is there an approved technology stack?
6. **Integrations:** What external systems or APIs must we integrate with?
7. **Compliance:** Are there regulatory, security, or privacy requirements?
8. **Scale:** What is the expected user load or data volume?
9. **Prior Art:** Are there competitors or existing solutions?
10. **Acceptance:** Who will make the final acceptance decision?
11. **Out of Scope:** What should explicitly not be included?
12. **Risks:** What are the biggest risks or unknowns?
